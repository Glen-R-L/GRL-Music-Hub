Minding a creative gap, this is the music of Glen R Levett.


Glen is a Christian Musician based in London. He plays Guitar, Bass, Keyboard and occasional Harmonica. He also sings and writes/produces his own homemade tracks.

Since 2005, he has written and recorded a wealth of Christian songs with a Pop Rock style. Lyrically, these songs generally have a semi autobiographical or topical approach reflecting on the highs and lows of being a Christian in the world as we know it.

Glen has also started recording experimental pop tracks under the name Mind The Gap , and previously recorded various novelty/comedy tracks under the name Novelty Hangouts Music between 2002 and 2019. In addition he's recorded with local Christian Rock band Stop and the Isle of Wight Gospel Choir .
